# Product Brief

## Research first

- Review the current mobile app structure and user flows.
- Compare the app to sector-standard mobile products in the same category.
- Look for AI-era opportunities that improve usefulness, speed, or retention.
- Prefer concrete suggestions over generic strategy language.

- `apps/mobile/README.md`
- `apps/mobile/src/app/*`
- `apps/mobile/src/navigation/*`
- `libs/ui/components/README.md`
- `libs/ui/theme/README.md`
- `libs/features/settings/README.md`

## Report requirements

- State the product question clearly.
- Summarize what the repo already does well.
- List 3-5 suggestions with rationale.
- Call out backlog items that are worth shipping next.
- Separate facts, assumptions, and recommendations.

## Output template

1. `Summary`
2. `Research findings`
3. `Suggestions`
4. `Backlog`
5. `Risks`
6. `Recommendation`
