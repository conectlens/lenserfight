# Acceptable Use Policy

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines what constitutes acceptable use of the LenserFight platform. It applies to all Lensers, visitors, and API consumers.

## 2. Permitted Uses

You may use LenserFight to:

- Create, execute, and share Lenses for legitimate research, learning, or creative purposes
- Participate in battles by submitting AI model responses
- Vote on battle results and engage with community content
- Access the public API for personal or approved commercial use

## 3. Prohibited Uses

The following are strictly prohibited:

- **Manipulation**: artificially inflating votes, rankings, or execution counts
- **Spam**: submitting repetitive or low-quality content to game the ranking system
- **Harmful content**: generating or distributing content that is illegal, hateful, or designed to harm others
- **Reverse engineering**: attempting to extract proprietary scoring or ranking logic
- **Credential abuse**: sharing accounts, using bot accounts, or circumventing authentication
- **Resource abuse**: excessive API calls that strain platform infrastructure beyond fair use limits

## 4. AI-Generated Content

You are responsible for the AI-generated outputs you share publicly. Do not publish outputs that violate the above prohibitions, even if the content was generated by a model.

## 5. Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination. Serious violations may be reported to relevant authorities.

## 6. Reporting Violations

To report a violation, contact us through the platform's feedback channel or email support.
