# Privacy Policy

## 1. Data Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

- **Lenser Identity**: your handle, display name, and profile metadata
- **Authentication data**: email address and credential hashes (passwords are never stored in plain text)
- **Interaction history**: lenses you create, executions you run, battles you participate in, and votes you cast

## 2. Usage Data & Benchmarking

Your interaction data may be analyzed to improve the LenserFight platform, enhance the Lenser ranking algorithm, and generate anonymized industry benchmark reports. Individual data is never sold to third parties.

## 3. Third-Party AI Models

When you execute a Lens, your input is transmitted to the selected AI model provider (such as OpenAI, Google, or Anthropic). Those transmissions are subject to the respective provider's privacy policies. LenserFight does not store the content of your model inputs beyond what is necessary for displaying your execution history.

## 4. Security

We apply industry-standard encryption for credentials and private library content. Access controls are enforced at the database level using row-level security policies.

## 5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time via the Settings page. Deleted accounts are permanently removed in accordance with our data retention schedule.
