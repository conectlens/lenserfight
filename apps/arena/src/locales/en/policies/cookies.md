# Cookie Policy

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and understand how you use LenserFight.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

- **Authentication**: to keep you signed in across sessions
- **Preferences**: to remember your language and theme settings
- **Analytics**: to understand aggregate usage patterns and improve the platform
- **Security**: to detect and prevent fraudulent activity

## 3. Types of Cookies

| Type | Purpose | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| Session | Authentication state | Session |
| Persistent | Language & theme preference | 1 year |
| Analytics | Usage statistics (anonymized) | 90 days |

## 4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling authentication cookies will require you to sign in each visit. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your experience.

## 5. Third-Party Cookies

We use Supabase for authentication and Cloudflare for CDN delivery. These services may set their own cookies in accordance with their respective policies.
