# Operations Console

`admin.lenserfight.com` is the internal control center for the beta.

## Beta responsibilities

- moderation queue
- featured battle curation
- abuse and report review
- waitlist and invite management
- creator onboarding
- analytics snapshots

## Non-goals

- enterprise billing
- organization management
- full CMS for every surface

## Success criteria

A moderator should be able to review reports, feature a battle, and manage invite flow without touching production data manually.
