# Join the Beta

The April-May 2026 beta is optimized for creators, indie AI builders, and early community members.

## Access model

- Public visitors can browse public battle pages and community content.
- Account creation is open for reading and lightweight participation when enabled.
- Battle creation, featured posting, and moderator actions are invite-gated.

## Best first steps

1. Read [Overview](/tutorials/getting-started/overview).
2. Understand [How Battles Work](/explanation/battle-system/how-battles-work).
3. Review [Hybrid Scoring](/explanation/battle-system/hybrid-scoring).
4. Join forum categories that match your interests.

## Beta expectations

- Features may be intentionally narrow to protect launch dates.
- Mobile supports the core loop only.
- Team battles, private workspaces, and rich tournament tools are later-phase work.
