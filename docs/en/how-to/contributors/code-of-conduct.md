# Code of Conduct

This page explains the community standards expected in LenserFight spaces.

## Our expectation

LenserFight should be respectful, constructive, and safe for contributors of all experience levels.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind, clear, and collaborative.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Ask clarifying questions before assuming intent.
- Respect boundaries, time zones, and different levels of experience.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Persistent disruption, trolling, or bad-faith engagement

## Reporting

- If the issue is security-sensitive, follow the [Security Policy](/en/how-to/contributors/security).
- Otherwise, report the behavior through a safe maintainer channel or a GitHub issue when public reporting is appropriate.

Include what happened, where it happened, who was involved, and any relevant evidence that helps maintainers review the report.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take action needed to protect the community, including warnings, content removal, temporary bans, or permanent bans.
