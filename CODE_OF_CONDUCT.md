# Code of Conduct

LenserFight should be a respectful, constructive, and harassment-free community for everyone.

This root file exists for GitHub’s community health features. The canonical contributor version lives at [docs/en/how-to/contributors/code-of-conduct.md](docs/en/how-to/contributors/code-of-conduct.md).

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Ask clarifying questions before assuming intent.
- Give actionable feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Respect boundaries, time zones, and differing levels of experience.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Persistent disruption, trolling, or bad-faith engagement.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. If it is security-sensitive, follow `SECURITY.md`.
2. Otherwise, report it through a safe maintainer channel or, when appropriate, a GitHub issue.

Include:
- what happened
- where it happened (issue/PR/link)
- who was involved
- any relevant screenshots or logs

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take action they deem appropriate, including warnings, content removal, temporary bans, or permanent bans, to protect the community.
